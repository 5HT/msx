Title: Montezuma's Revenge
Environment: MSX or newer, Keyboard & Joypad, Musical Memory Mapper Cartridge
Genre: Platform
Language: English
Year: 1984
Publisher: Parker Bros.
Developer: Parker Bros. (Designed by Robert Jaeger)

Notes:
- Start the game with ROM2MMM.COM under MSX-DOS 1/2, or from a plain ROM of
  same size.
- Conversion by GDX.
- The MSX with a Russian keyboard are not supported.
- crc32 = ce9544ba

Keys Mapping:

 F5           -  Reset game
 Cursor keys  -  Movement of player 1
 SPACE        -  Jump
 N            -  Jump
 0-8          -  Level and player number select

Joysticks:

 Joystick 1   -  Movement of player 1
 Joystick 2   -  Movement of player 2
 Button 1     -  Jump
 Button 2     -  Jump
